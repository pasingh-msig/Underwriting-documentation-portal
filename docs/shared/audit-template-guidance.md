# Audit Template Guidance

## Purpose
This page explains the role of each audit-oriented template in the underwriting documentation portal and how teams should use them during documentation, governance, and audit preparation.

## Control Mapping
The Control Mapping document is the primary traceability document used to connect audit requirements, control expectations, and documentation evidence.

## Evidence Index
The Evidence Index is the inventory of supporting artifacts used to demonstrate that controls, policies, and governance expectations are operating as intended.

## Risk Register
The Risk Register captures material risks associated with an initiative, platform, application, cutover, or control environment.

## Decision Log
The Decision Log documents key decisions that affect architecture, scope, delivery, controls, cutover approach, ownership, or operating model.

## Compliance and Policy Mapping
The Compliance and Policy Mapping document shows how an initiative, application, or platform aligns to applicable corporate policies, standards, and governance requirements.

Use it to connect:
- the policy or standard
- the specific requirement
- where it applies
- how it is addressed
- where supporting evidence is located
- who owns ongoing compliance

## Cutover Readiness Pack
The Cutover Readiness Pack documents whether a release, onboarding, migration, or phased rollout is ready to move into production.

## Benefits Realization Plan
The Benefits Realization Plan documents the business value expected from an initiative and how that value will be measured over time.

## Audit RACI
The Audit RACI defines who is responsible, accountable, consulted, and informed for audit-related activities, evidence preparation, and control response.

## Legacy Retirement and Source-of-Record Mapping
This document explains the relationship between the target-state platform or initiative and existing systems.
